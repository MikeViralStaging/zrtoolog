Pneumatic line

Pneumatic sealless tools.

Production speed without seals. Two buttons control the entire cycle — tensioning, sealing and cutting — on the same patented sealless joint the manual line is known for. Light for their class, easy to load, minutes to service.

Pneumatic · Sealless combination

PS25

Air-powered speed for production strapping. Two buttons run the whole cycle — tension, seal, cut — up to 1,800 lbs of pull.

1/2" × .015" (13 mm × 0.38 mm) to 3/4" × .025" (19 mm × 0.635 mm)

Pneumatic · Sealless combination

PS31

Heavier-gauge pneumatic strapping on 3/4" strap — 2,100 lbs of tension from the same two-button cycle.

3/4" × .025" (19 mm × 0.635 mm) to 3/4" × .031" (19 mm × 0.80 mm)

Pneumatic · Sealless combination

PS90

The big pull: 2,900 lbs of tension on 1 1/4" strap. Two buttons, one sealless joint, built for the heaviest packages.

1 1/4" × .025" (32 mm × 0.635 mm) to 1 1/4" × .031" (32 mm × 0.80 mm)

▸ Good to know

PS-series tools run on standard shop air — 65 psi for the PS25 and PS31, 80 psi for the PS90 — and are designed for the rapid, reliable strapping of concrete, crates, lumber, metal and palletized products.
