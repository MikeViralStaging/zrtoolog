Manual sealless combination tool

TE90.

Only one of its kindDrum / windlass sealless combination tool · Z-Lock · 1 1/4"

The heaviest pull in the manual line: drum / windlass tensioning on 1 1/4" strap, with Z-Lock reverse locking standard.

The TE90 pairs the widest strap in the manual line — 1 1/4" (32 mm) — with a drum / windlass tensioning system built for heavy-duty pull. It is the only combination tool of its kind, anywhere.

Like every ZL and TE tool, it ships with Z-Lock reverse locking, holds joints in either direction, and keeps effort minimal for a tool in this class. Small, round and irregular packages are exactly what it was designed around.

Designed for high-tensile steel strapping up to 156,000 psi (1,100 N/mm²). Runs regular-duty strap too — with durability to spare.

Why this tool

Built the ZR way.

  • Drum / windlass tensioning. Winds strap onto a drum for heavy-duty, controlled tension on the largest loads.
  • Only one of its kind. The only combination tool for 1 1/4" (32 mm) steel strapping with windlass tensioning.
  • Z-Lock reverse locking. Our exclusive Z-Lock system protects the sealless connection against loosening or unlocking in either direction — built for the most demanding loads.
  • Fewest parts in its class. Comparable tools carry roughly twice as many components. Fewer parts means easier operation, less maintenance, fewer repairs.
  • Minutes to service. Feedwheel, gripper, knife, punch or die — each swaps out in a few minutes with basic hand tools.
  • All-steel construction. Every part manufactured and treated for long service life under real shop conditions.
  • Built for North American strap. Designed around North American-grade steel strapping. Performs even better on European-grade strap.
